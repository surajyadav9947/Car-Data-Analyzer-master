Project DriveSense
The OBD reader(On Board Diagnostics) can read important car data like battery,car engine temperature, odometer readings,Gear Selection and GPS which can be used for evaluating the driver and car performance which can be practically implemented with cars manufactured on or after 2008.

In this analysis we have simulated sample data which resembles the parameters given by the OBD device and using which the data has been processed accordingly.
5 cars were considered after our research and their specific data can be found on the header.
The 5 chosen cars were
  1. Tata Harrier
  2. Mahindra XUV500
  3. MG Hector
  4. Toyota Fortuner
  5. KIA Seltos
